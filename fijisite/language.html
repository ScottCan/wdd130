Moce - Goodbye

Rua - Two

Va - Four

Lima - Five

Ono - Six

Vitu - Seven

Walu - Eight

Ciwa - Nine

Tini - Ten

Phrases

Na yacaqu o ___ - My name is ___

O cei na yacamu? - What is your name?

Au mai ___ - I am from ___

Ko mai vei? - Where are you from?

Vakacava tiko? - How are you?

Vakacava na nomu siga nikua? - How is your day?

Set tiko - I am doing good

Au sa viakana - I am hungry

Au sa oca - I am tired

E rawa meu wasea e dua na itukutuku? - Can I share a message?

Pronounciation

a as in father

e as in bet

i as in pink

o as in don't

u as in glue

The other letters make the same sound as in English, except for the following:

b is mb

c is th

d is nd

g is a soft ng as in sing

j is ch

q is a hard ng as in angry

r is rolled
